Using a TEG generator to convert Excess Heat into Electricity

I'm building a rocket mass heater and want to power a light and maybe my computer. a) Do I need a charger converter? b)What specifications are important for the TEG I get, such as Max Temp, Max Power output etc?

a) Converters and inverters do the oppposite of each other, both converting between DC and AC current. Almost all appliances run off AC and because Peltier Plates output DC so you'll want probably want an inverter, unless you're using a basic lamp that runs off DC. A computer would definitely need AC. b)

An aside: Thermal energy released from wood is about: 1.6 - 5 kWh/kg depending on moisture content (1).
